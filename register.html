Enrollment Requirements

Before proceeding to payment, please ensure you meet these requirements:

  • You are currently enrolled in Grade 9, 10, 11, or 12
  • You have access to a reliable internet connection
  • You have a device (computer, tablet, or smartphone) for learning
  • You are committed to following the study schedule
  • You have a valid payment method (Telebirr, CBE, Awash Bank, or Dashen Bank)
